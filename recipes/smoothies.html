Smoothie

Mixed berries and banana smoothie

This four-ingredient smoothie only takes a few minutes to blend and it serves two!

Ingredient

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 frozen ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Steps

  1. First cut berries and banana into pieces
  2. Add pieces into the blender
  3. Add vanilla yogurt and orange juice into the blender
  4. honey is optional
  5. Blend it smoothly
  6. Add some ice cubes and enjoy.
